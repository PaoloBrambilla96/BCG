KEY SUPPLIERS

  • Materials’ supplier, that provides all the materials needed for operations.
  • Petrol refueling, that is fundamental for the fleet of vehicles that must be always ready to respond immediately in case of emergencies. Currently, Eldy keeps at stock a quantity of fuel sufficient for 20 days of operations.
  • Fleet supplier, that delivers new vehicles in case of need.

EXTERNAL DEPENDENCIES

  • Electricity transmission phase, whose disruption determines a disruption also of the medium and low voltage lines.
  • Telecommunication, whose disruption interrupts all the information flows, the digital monitoring of the network and the metering.
  • Road transportation network, whose disruption doesn’t allow the emergency response team to intervene immediately in case of issues and problems in the distribution network.
