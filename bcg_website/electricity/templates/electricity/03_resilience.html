4Rs approach
Risk prevention
Actions able to minimize the probability of occurrence of the negative effects resulting from extreme natural events. In particular, based on the risk index associated to each voltage line, Eldy prioritizes the lines that require most urgent interventions.
Readiness
Actions to increase the forecasting and monitoring capacity of the network in extreme natural conditions, also by collaborating with other public utility companies and institutions.
Response
Planning, execution and communication during the emergency. Interaction with citizens is crucial and essential for the success of the actions implemented by the distribution companies.
Recovery
Ability of the electricity distribution network to quickly return to ordinary operating conditions. In particular, network interconnections increase resilience by providing alternative power supply routes.
